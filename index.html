About Artificial Intelligence Division

Keeping tab of latest courses that would nurture students in their career and aid them in achieving long term goals, the School of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at VIT Bhopal University – one of the first and best engineering institutes in India is offering four programs

School of Computing Science & Engineering (SCSE) offers B.Tech and M.Tech (Integrated) in Artificial Intelligence to prospective students. Today, AI is changing and influencing the global perspective like never before. The faculty is determined and dedicated to ensure that potential. The division encourages students to explore and delve deeper into the world of Artificial Intelligence.
